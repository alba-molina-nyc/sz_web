About Us

Szuster Psychiatry Team

We have been serving the Jackson Heights and greater New York City area since 2003. Our team is comprised of dedicated psychiatrists, psychiatric nurse practitioners and clinical social workers. We provide psychiatry and psychotherapy for individuals, families and couples.
Dr. David Szuster

We specialize in diagnosing and treating a wide range of psychiatric disorders. Dr. Szuster is an expert in child & adolescent psychiatry.


As a prescribing doctor, we provide psychiatric services in English and Spanish and have years of experience in their respective specialties. We also offer a variety of group therapy options which include anxiety management, mindfulness/meditation, and immigration experience groups run in Spanish.
