About Us

Szuster Psychiatry Team

Dr. David Szuster

We have been serving the Jackson Heights and greater New York City area since 2003. Our team is comprised of dedicated psychiatrists, psychiatric nurse practitioners and clinical social workers. We provide psychiatry and psychotherapy for individuals, families and couples. We offer services in Spanish and English and accept most insurance plans..


We specialize in diagnosing and treating a wide range of psychiatric disorders. Dr. Szuster is an expert in child & adolescent psychiatry and Dr. Millet specializes in working with geriatric populations. Our therapists have training in many modalities including CBT, DBT, Family Systems, and Psychoanalysis.


All three of our prescribing clinicians provide psychiatric services in English and Spanish and have years of experience in their respective specialties. We also offer a variety of group therapy options which include anxiety management, mindfulness/meditation, and immigration experience groups run in Spanish. Please visit our website for further information: www.drszuster.com
